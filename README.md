# Privacy Policy for Patterns: Habit Tracker
**Last updated: July 5, 2026**
This Privacy Policy describes how Patterns: Habit Tracker ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.
## Information We Collect
**Information you provide directly:**
- Habit names, icons, and frequency settings you create
- Optional personal notes (e.g. your "why this matters" entries)
- Your reminder time preferences
**Information collected automatically:**
- Streak and completion history (generated by your use of the app)
- Approximate time-of-day patterns for missed/completed habits, used to generate in-app insights
- Device type and operating system version (for compatibility and bug fixing)
- Push notification token (to deliver your reminders)
**Payment information:** If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment card details.
## How We Use Your Information
- To provide the core functionality of the app (tracking habits, streaks, and reminders)
- To generate the in-app insight cards (e.g. noticing you tend to miss evening habits)
- To send you the reminder notifications you've set
- To diagnose and fix technical issues
- To process subscription payments through the App Store/Play Store
We do not sell your personal information.
## Data Storage
All habit data, notes, and history are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information unless you choose to contact us directly (e.g. for support).

## Your Rights
You can:

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it is retained for as long as the app remains installed. Deleting the app, or clearing its data/an individual habit within the app, removes that data immediately.
